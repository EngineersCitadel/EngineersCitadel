Products / Services

Design and Manufacturing support of Solar Module Mounting structure (Roof top systems)

  • Selection of right MMS for the application.
  • Structure stability analysis (STAAD Pro) for the wind loads.
  • Design of corrosion protection for the structure.
  • Design of base anchoring for the structure.

Design and Manufacturing support of Elevated Solar Module mounting structure (Roof top systems)

  • Stability analysis (STAAD Pro) of structure against wind loads.
  • Design of walkways & maintenance cat walk.
  • Design of anchoring system & foundations.

Design of Solar Module mounting structure (Ground mounted systems)

  • Design & analysis (STAAD Pro) of structure for the required tilt angle against wind loads.
  • Design & selection of suitable coating for corrosion protection of structure.
  • Design of Pile foundations for the site, to suite the soil condition.

Design of structure for Solar Car ports (Ground mounted & MLCP)

  • Design of custom build Solar Car port.
  • Design of maintenance walk ways & plumbing.
  • Design of suitable foundations to suite the soil condition.

Design of single axis solar tracker mechanisms

  • Design of structure & tracker mechanism.
  • Design of Hydraulic or Electrical actuator mechanisms.
  • Tracker control system selection.
  • Design of foundation to suite the soil condition.

Design & development of Aluminium extrusions

  • Design of custom sections to meet different applications.
  • Selection of suitable Aluminum alloy material to meet the application requirements.
  • Customization of standard sections available to meet varied requirements.

Design & analysis of steel chimney structures

  • Structure stability verification against wind loads & seismic loads (STAAD Pro).
  • Structure weight optimization.
  • Foundation sizing verification to suit the soil condition.

Roof stability verification of industrial sheds for Solar

  • Roof truss stability verification against external wind loads, live loads & solar loads.
  • Design to strengthen existing roofs to take additional loads.

Design of steel structure building

  • Design of steel mezzanine floor.
  • Design of foundations, stairs & roof truss.

Design of steel stair case

  • Design & analysis (STAAD-Pro) of high raise industrial stair case.

Design of small CED process plant

Design of water pumping & pipe lines
